Your Go-To Guide of
Food Places Around Tech

From an unprofessionally certified student.

Hawaii Poke Bowl

My Rating: 10/10

I love Hawaii Poke Bowl. Eel, spicy tuna, spicy salmon, and
chicken are all good proteins. I also get cilantro, sweet corn,
cucumber, edamame, spicy crab salad, mango, pineapple, cilantro ranch,
house poke sauce, and sweet unagi sauce.

Chick N' Burger

My Rating: 9/10

Chick N' Burger is pretty good too. I usually get the
crispy chicken burger and fries. The popcorn chicken is yummy as well
but I am not a huge fan of their bentos. I would recommend eating
their food right away so it does not lose its crisp.

Chipotle

My Rating: 8.5/10

Chipotle is a classic that has been around for a while.
You cannot go wrong with it, I like extra cheese and guacamole is
delicious except I am cheap and do not like paying extra. Chips
are also enjoyable, maybe too salty sometimes but still good.
